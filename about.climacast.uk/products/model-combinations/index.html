Products/Model Combinations
Product / 01

More than one model. One clearer signal.

ClimaCast compares several forecasts and explains where they agree, where uncertainty remains, and what has changed.

Explore the product Visit ClimaCast

At a glance

  • PurposeMake forecast confidence easier to understand.
  • ShowsAgreement, uncertainty and meaningful changes.
  • OutputA short, plain-language signal.
Why it exists

One forecast can look certain when the wider evidence is not. ClimaCast keeps that uncertainty visible.

01 / What it does

Confidence, without the chart-reading.

The technical comparison happens underneath. The page stays focused on the few signals that help someone make a decision.

01

Shows where forecasts agree.

A clear summary explains whether timing, intensity and movement are becoming more settled.

02

Keeps uncertainty honest.

Important disagreement remains visible when it could change the outcome or the advice.

03

Highlights what changed.

Each update says whether confidence has strengthened, weakened or stayed broadly the same.

02 / How it works

Technical underneath. Simple on the surface.

01

Compare the forecasts.

Relevant model guidance is considered together rather than presenting one forecast as the whole picture.

02

Find the shared signal.

ClimaCast identifies agreement and the differences that could materially change the outcome.

03

Explain it plainly.

The result is a short statement of confidence, change and what the evidence means for the decision.

The outcome

Less comparison. Better judgement.

Model Combinations gives people a calmer view of forecast confidence without asking them to study several technical charts.
